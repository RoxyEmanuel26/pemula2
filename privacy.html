By accessing and using this site, you express your consent to the gathering and processing of information in accordance with this documented policy.

📝Š Information We Collect

1. Automated Device Logging

When you visit our site, we automatically log specific metadata from your web browser, including:

  • IP Address — collected for security diagnostics and analytics
  • Browser Type and Version — collected to ensure display layout compatibility
  • Operating System — analyzed for platform speed optimizations
  • Visited Pages — utilized to analyze overall user engagements
  • Access Timestamps — logged to track security server events
  • Referrer URLs — used to understand inbound network traffic channels

2. Cookies

We use small local cookies to manage site properties, such as:

  • Theme Preferences — preserves your selected dark/light visual mode (kumpulenak_theme)
  • Browsing Sessions — retains internal state structures during navigation
  • Advertising Cookies — our third-party ad networks (Monetag, Adsterra) set tracking cookies to serve relevant advertisements

3. Information You Directly Provide

If you contact us via email or submit our digital contact form, we collect:

  • Your full name and email address
  • The textual contents of the message you sent

🔒 How We Use Your Information

The logged information is utilized solely to:

  • Provide and maintain our overall website infrastructure
  • Analyze and evaluate traffic patterns to improve site properties
  • Deliver advertisements via our third-party marketing channels
  • Respond to user support claims and DMCA takedown requests
  • Detect and prevent abusive activity or malicious exploit attempts
  • Comply with prevailing global data protection laws

🤝 Third-Party Data Sharing

We **do not sell** or lease your personal information. However, we collaborate with third-party networks who may collect anonymized traffic data through cookies:

  • Monetag — adult advertising and monetization platform
  • Adsterra — display banner marketing network
  • Vercel — cloud hosting architecture and CDN provider
  • Eporner API — external media catalog provider

Each of these partners maintains their own independent privacy policy governing data usage.

🍪 Managing Cookie Settings

You can adjust, block, or delete cookies at any time inside your web browser:

  • Google Chrome: Settings ←’ Privacy and Security ←’ Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings ←’ Privacy & Security ←’ Cookies and Site Data
  • Apple Safari: Preferences ←’ Privacy ←’ Cookies and website data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings ←’ Cookies and site permissions ←’ Manage and delete cookies

Note: Disabling cookies entirely may limit some local features (e.g., your selected light/dark mode preference will reset upon closing the browser).

If you discover a minor has shared personal data with us, contact support immediately for absolute data deletion.
