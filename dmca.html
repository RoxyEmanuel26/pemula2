DMCA Takedown Policy

Copyright Infringement & Content Removal Guidelines

📝‹ About DMCA

kumpulenak respects the intellectual property rights of content creators and expects users of this platform to do the same. We respond strictly to notices of alleged copyright infringement in accordance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).

If you believe that your copyrighted work has been copied or distributed on this platform in a manner that constitutes infringement, please follow the formal claim procedures outlined below.

📝 DMCA Claim Procedure

To file an official takedown request, please email us with the following required information:

1

Identification of the copyrighted work that you claim has been infringed.

2

Direct URLs or specific links to the infringing material on our platform.

3

Contact information including your full name, email address, physical address, and telephone number.

4

A good-faith statement that the use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the governing law.

5

An electronic signature from the copyright holder or the person authorized to act on behalf of the owner.

📧 Send DMCA Request

Send your DMCA takedown notice directly to:

📧 dmca@kumpulenak.my.id

Our copyright team will review and respond to valid claims within **3-5 business days**.

⚡ Takedown Processing Workflow

  • Upon receiving a valid DMCA request, we will **immediately review** the copyright claim.
  • If the claim is verified, the infringing content will be **removed within 3-5 business days**.
  • The claimant will receive a **confirmation email** once the removal process is completed.
  • Repeat copyright offenders will be **permanently banned** from uploading or hosting galleries on this platform.
